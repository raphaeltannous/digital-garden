Welcome to my digital garden1. The place, where I share my notes and knowledge in digital form for everyone. I mainly write these notes for myself. For example, LeetCode Solutions holds the thought process while solving the LeetCode problem at hand.

The digital garden has no AI generated content. Each line in the digital garden is written by me. I do not care whether you use AI. This digital garden will stay written by me, and I do not want to remove the human factor from the equation. That’s why I will not use any AI proofreading for my notes. They are presented as is, proofread by my human friends and (hopefully) by you.

I want to keep this digital garden as simple as possible. I think you’ve noticed that there’s no search bar! Yes, I am aware. You can clone all of my notes from codeberg, and search the text using grep or any other search tool. If you do not have git, codeberg provides ways to install this digital garden.

You can reach me via raphaeltannous@proton.me, or using Signal via Signal Link or searching for raphaeltannous.01 as the username.

Happy Learning!
